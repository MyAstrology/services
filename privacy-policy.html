2. Information We Collect

A. Registration & Booking Information

During booking and consultation, MyAstrology collects your name, gender, date of birth, time of birth, place of birth and phone number. This information is used to calculate your horoscope accurately. Clear photographs of both palms are collected via WhatsApp for palmistry analysis. You are not required to provide personal information simply to browse the website.

B. IP Address and Cookies

When you visit myastrology.in, MyAstrology and third-party service providers may collect information about your browsing activity including IP address, browser type, screen resolution, pages visited and time spent on site. This data is used in aggregate to improve site performance and user experience and is not used to personally identify individual visitors.

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand visitor behaviour. This data is anonymised and does not personally identify you. You may opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

C. Order Form Information

We collect information necessary to complete the services provided through our booking forms. We guarantee strict confidentiality of our members' identities, birth details and predictions. MyAstrology will not use any information contained in the predictions for any purpose other than delivering the booked service to the member.

D. Payment Information

We do not collect or store any payment information (card numbers, CVV, UPI credentials or banking passwords) on our servers. All payments are processed securely by Razorpay, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment gateway licensed by the Reserve Bank of India. A payment receipt including the Payment ID is sent to the customer via WhatsApp as confirmation.

E. WhatsApp Communications

Information shared via WhatsApp — including palm photographs, birth details and floor plans for Vastu consultations — is used solely to deliver the requested service. This information is not shared with any third party. Phone and video consultations are not recorded.

F. Other Information

Any information received via email or other communication channels relating to specific services is used only to address your specific needs and is not added to any member profile or shared externally.

3. Security

We do not collect payment information on our website. All payment data is handled by Razorpay using industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS). We follow reasonable technical and procedural measures to protect the personal information you provide against unauthorised access, use or disclosure.

Important: While we take all reasonable precautions, no website or Internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure. We are committed to maintaining your trust and take your data security seriously.

4. Links to Other Websites
